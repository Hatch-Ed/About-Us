About Us

As per the National Education Policy 2020, programming will become a compulsory subject in all schools from class 6 onwards. Hence, there can exist a platform that is entirely dedicated towards school students, so that by the time they pass out of the school, they are already acquainted with the latest technology and skill sets, pertaining to this field.

Hence, Hatch.Ed intends on filling that void gap which has been created due to the current changes in education system. We have a team of inquisitive college minds, recruited after a tough interview process, to provide mentorship and guidance to the school students.
